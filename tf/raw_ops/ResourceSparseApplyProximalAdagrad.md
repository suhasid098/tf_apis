That is for rows we have grad for, we update var and accum as follows:
accum += grad * grad
prox_v = var
prox_v -= lr * grad * (1 / sqrt(accum))
var = sign(prox_v)/(1+lr*l2) * max{|prox_v|-lr*l1,0}
