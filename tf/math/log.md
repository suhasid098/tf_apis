I.e., \\(y = \log_e x\\).

#### Example:


```
>>> x = tf.constant([0, 0.5, 1, 5])
>>> tf.math.log(x)
<tf.Tensor: shape=(4,), dtype=float32, numpy=array([      -inf, -0.6931472,  0.       ,  1.609438 ], dtype=float32)>
```
